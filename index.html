About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer with 9 years of experience delivering technology solutions across engineering, automation, data systems, and web development. My career began in renewable energy and industrial automation, where I developed a strong foundation in problem-solving, system design, and building reliable solutions for complex operational environments.

After relocating to Canada, I expanded into software development by creating internal business applications, workflow automations, nd data management solutions using Power Apps, SharePoint, Power Automate, and TypeScript. These projects helped organizations streamline processes, reduce manual effort, and improve operational efficiency while strengthening my expertise in modern software development.

Since then, I have designed and delivered 40+ websites, web applications, and digital platforms for businesses and public companies. My experience spans front-end development, backend integrations, database-driven applications, API development, workflow automation, and responsive user interface design. I enjoy building solutions that combine clean user experiences with scalable and maintainable architecture.

My technical expertise includes JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Node.js, HTML, CSS, REST APIs, PostgreSQL, SharePoint, Power Platform, and modern development workflows. I am passionate about creating applications that solve real-world problems, automate business processes, and provide meaningful value to users.

Bilingual in English and French, I thrive in collaborative environments where technology, business needs, and user experience come together to create impactful digital solutions.
