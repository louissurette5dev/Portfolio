About Me

I’m a Front-End Developer with 9 years of experience spanning engineering, automation, data tools, and modern web development. My career began in renewable energy and industrial automation, where I strengthened my problem-solving mindset and worked on systems requiring precision and reliability.

After relocating to Canada, I moved into a bilingual client-facing role where I supported customers in both English and French. During that time, I developed a strong interest in technology and was given opportunities to automate workflows and build internal tools using Power Apps, SharePoint, and Power Automate. These projects eventually led me to build custom SPFx applications using TypeScript, which solidified my path toward front-end development. I’ve also designed and delivered 40+ websites on Wix, including AGM and Special Meeting microsites used by public companies for shareholder communications. These projects strengthened my skills in responsive design, UX, and fast-turnaround development for high-visibility events.

I’m currently completing the Full-Stack Development Certificate at Toronto Metropolitan University, expanding my skills in React, Node.js, API integration, and modern development workflows.

Bilingual in English and French, I’m passionate about creating responsive, user-centered interfaces that improve efficiency and deliver clear, meaningful experiences.
